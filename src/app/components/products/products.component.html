Storage & Cooking Tips

Store in a paper bag in the refrigerator. Avoid plastic bags as they trap moisture. Use within 3-5 days for best freshness.

Wipe with a damp cloth or use a soft brush. Avoid soaking in water as mushrooms absorb moisture.

Great for stir-fries, soups, curries, grilling, or sautéing. Cook on medium-high heat to bring out their natural flavors.
