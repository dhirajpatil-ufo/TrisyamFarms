About Trishyam Farms

Trishyam Farms is a mushroom farming venture focused on producing premium Oyster Mushrooms using modern and eco-friendly methods.

Located in the fertile lands of Sangli, Maharashtra, we are committed to providing fresh, healthy, and chemical-free mushrooms while maintaining consistent quality throughout the year. Our farm combines traditional farming wisdom with modern techniques to deliver the best produce to our customers.

To revolutionize mushroom farming by providing consistently high-quality, chemical-free Oyster Mushrooms through sustainable and transparent farming practices. We aim to promote healthy eating while supporting local communities.

  • Create employment opportunities
